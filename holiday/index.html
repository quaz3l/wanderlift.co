Frequently Asked Questions

What resorts will rides be available to?

Destinations are determined by our drivers, but you can expect to see regular rides to popular resorts like Keystone, Beaver Creek, and Vail.

Who am I driving up to the mountains with?

Wanderlift drivers are other students and users from your community. Communities include the University of Denver, and CU Boulder.

Will there be other people in the car?

Sometimes. You can book with a friend and go together. There may be other students in your ride as well who have also booked seats.

How is the gift certificate redeemed?

The money is credited immediately to the student’s name and phone number, then translated to their account when they signup.
